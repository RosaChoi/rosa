Architect's Artist

Architect's Artist Wasel Choi is an established artist whose artwork has successfully grown to encompass his passion for art and architecture. He enjoys composing sharp contrasting volumes, shapes and colors on a large canvas and three-dimensional objects. Wasel has partnered with some of the big international brands to create collaborative art works for public and private clients, including Nissan, CapitaLand and Formula 1. His works are in the permanent collections of corporations, museums, hotels, cultural organizations, residential developments and dignitaries.

Wasel Choi commissioned Rosa to develop a visual identity and website that presents the whole spectrum of his art career and provides a platform for his other projects.
