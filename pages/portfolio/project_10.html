pocketSOHO

pocketSOHO is a Web App that empowers Small/Home business owners to run their business smoothly and efficiently. pocketSOHO enables users to communicate with their customers 24/7; schedule and keep track of appointments; and send invoices, track payments, get tax reporting done at their fingertips. pocketSOHO is a consolidated, on the go solution for busy individuals who are in charge of their business.
The goal was to create a brand identity for pocketSOHO to attract potential users and engage the right message to the audiences from the start.
